QGIS Training (Beginner)

Who is it for

Users with minimal to zero GIS knowledge, veteran GIS users who want to learn QGIS, & everyone in between.

What to expect

By the end of the training, you will know the basic concepts of GIS and geospatial data formats. You should also be familiar with the QGIS interface as well as its features and functionalities that will help you create, edit, manage, analyze, and visualize GIS data.

Duration

Approximately 16 hours (2 days)

Topics covered

Introduction to GIS
Introduction to QGIS
The QGIS Interface
QGIS Plugins
Data Layers & Remote Services
QGIS Data Management
Processing Tools
Basic Styling & Symbology
Expressions
Making Maps using the Print Layout

Certificates

The course is vetted by the QGIS community. If you opt for a certificate, you will receive a digital certificate with a unique certificate ID. The certificate fee is donated back to the QGIS project.
